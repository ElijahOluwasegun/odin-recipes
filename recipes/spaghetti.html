Spaghetti

Description

This is a recipe for spaghetti made by me

Ingredients

  • Salt
  • water
  • Onions (2)
  • Green Pepper(3)
  • Tomato (2)
  • Curry spice
  • White pepper spice
  • Coriander spice
  • Thyme spice

Steps

  1. Add the spaghetti to your cooking bowl
  2. Add one and a half tablespoon of salt
  3. Pour water to the level of the spaghetti in the bowl
  4. Chop your green pepper inside
  5. Turn on the gas or electric cooker to the highest heat level
  6. Add 1/4 tablespoon of curry, thyme, white pepper and coriander
  7. Chop the onions inside
  8. Wait for 20mins
  9. Add your cut tomatoes
  10. Wait for 5 mins
  11. Off the cooker and enjoy

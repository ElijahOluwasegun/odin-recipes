Fried rice

Description

This dish is popular among many cultures. However, this particular fried rice recipe that will be shown is native to Nigeria

Ingredients

  • Rice (4 cups)
  • Chicken water
  • Beef water
  • Onions (12)
  • Carrots (2)
  • Green pepper (2)
  • Curry spice
  • White pepper spice
  • Coriander spice
  • Thyme spice
  • Bay leaf

Steps

  1. Pour the rice in the bowl
  2. Add chicken water, beef water, 4 cut onions
  3. Add 1 tablespoon of salt, 1 tablespoon of curry spice, 1/2 of white pepper spice, coriander spice and thyme spice and 6 pieces of bay leaves
  4. Pour hot water up to rice level
  5. Stir, heat up and wait for 30 mins
  6. While waiting fry the vegetables in bowl. Cut 2 onions, 2 carrots, 1 green pepper
  7. Put carrots first in the bowl with oil (1/4 spoon) poured in and heated
  8. Stir and wait for 5 mins
  9. Then add green pepper
  10. Stir and wait for 5 mins
  11. Then add onions
  12. Stir and after 5 mins remove from bowl
  13. Add rice in the bowl you used to fry the vegetables
  14. Add more water
  15. Add 1/2 tablespoon of salt, 1/2 tablespoon of curry spice, 1/4 of white pepper spice, coriander spice and thyme spice and 4 pieces of bay leaves
  16. Add 3 more onions
  17. Stir and wait for 15 mins
  18. Off heater and enjoy
